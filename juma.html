JUMA

/dʒu·mə/

Juma is a farm-to-fork food truck operating in Victoria, B.C. bringing to life global flavours from local produce. Juma’s seasonal menu cycles through the bounty of fruits and vegetables, locally grown right here, on Vancouver Island. We also guarantee that 100% of the lamb, pork and chicken appearing on the menu is pasture-raised on our very own family farm in Saanich. If you want to eat amazing food, support local farming/agriculture and be more aware of where your food comes from, then look no further!
